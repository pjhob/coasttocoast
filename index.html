About Coast To Coast Refrigeration

Coast To Coast Refrigeration has over 40 years experience in the commercial and industrial refrigeration and air conditioning industries. A friendly family run business, established 26 years ago, we provide a quality service at a competitive price.

Breakdown and repair service:

Prompt call outs, out of hours monitored service, friendly and experienced engineers, covering Cornwall 24 hours a day all year round. With ever increasing electricity costs the efficient running of your refrigeration, agricultural plant and air conditioning is vital. We can offer maintenance contracts or set up a yearly schedule scheme to suit your requirements.

Refrigeration

We supply quality equipment with a minimum 2 years parts and labour warranty and all installations are carried out by our own engineers. Refrigeration is classed as hazard waste so we can provide a disposal service.

Serve over counters

Cold rooms and

Deep freeze rooms

Agriculture

Coast To Coast Refrigeration are the newly appointed Dairy Kool dealer for Cornwall and can provide new, refurbished and second hand bulk milk tanks. We repair and maintain all types of bulk milk tanks and can provide heat recovery systems to reduce your running costs. We install data loggers for your bulk milk tank so in the event of a break down it can notify you by SMS text or email. Our refrigeration maintenance contract covers all labour costs and two services a year. All you pay for are competitively priced parts if required.
